Add your sound files here!

Required Files:
--------------
1. background.mp3 - Your background music (will loop continuously at low volume)
2. meme1.mp3 - First meme sound
3. meme2.mp3 - Second meme sound
4. meme3.mp3 - Third meme sound

File Format:
-----------
- MP3 or WAV format
- Recommended duration for meme sounds: 1-5 seconds
- Background music can be any length (will loop automatically)

Tips:
-----
- Shorter meme sounds (1-3 seconds) work best
- Background music volume is automatically set to 20%
- Meme sounds play at 70% volume
- The meme sounds trigger when you get close to pipes!

Where to Get Sounds:
-------------------
- Download meme sounds from YouTube (use a youtube-to-mp3 converter)
- Record your own voice
- Use sound effects from free sources like Freesound.org
- Use music from royalty-free sites

Once you add these files, DELETE this README.txt file.
